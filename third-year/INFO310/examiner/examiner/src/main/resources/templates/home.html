Welcome to Examiner Finder

This site allows you to select and submit an examiner committee. Approval of your examiner committee is a mandatory component of your PhD or Masters qualification.

You must select three examiners – one from your department, one from New Zealand, and one international examiner. Please ensure there are no conflicts of interest in your submission. Once submitted, you will be notified once your department’s convenor has approved your committee. If you are required to make changes and resubmit, you will also be notified.
